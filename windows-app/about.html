About Game Tracker App

Welcome to the Game Tracker App! This app helps gamers track their gaming sessions, monitor playtime, and gather insights based on their gaming behavior. Whether you're a casual player or a serious gamer, this app provides valuable information on how you spend your time gaming.

With the Game Tracker App, you can monitor play sessions, visualize your game statistics, and even predict which games you might want to play next based on past behavior.

Key Features

  • Track playtime automatically across all games on your PC.
  • Generate detailed statistics, including Total Playtime, Longest Session, and Average Playtime Per Day.
  • Get insights on gaming patterns, such as Weekend vs. Weekday playtime.
  • View session history and performance trends over time.
  • Predict your next game based on historical data.
  • Backup and restore your session data to the cloud.
  • Seamless user authentication through Steam.
